# LEGEND D Engineering Constitution

## 1. Purpose
LEGEND D is not only an ERP interface. It is a business operating platform designed to support operations, management, and intelligence.

## 2. Core Principles
1. Data is entered once, then reused across the system.
2. Every business document must have a lifecycle, revision history, audit trail, and traceability links.
3. Every important decision must be explainable and logged.
4. AI must never replace the human decision maker; it recommends and explains.
5. Every screen must produce structured data that can support reporting, dashboards, and AI analysis.
6. Modules must share common engines instead of duplicating logic.

## 3. Document Rules
Every major document should support:
- Numbering Engine.
- Workflow status.
- Revision number.
- Audit Trail.
- Timeline.
- Attachments.
- Linked documents.
- Decision Log when approval or supplier selection is involved.

## 4. AI Rules
Every new feature should answer:
- What business event does this feature generate?
- Can the event be used by AI later?
- Is the data structured enough to explain a future decision?

## 5. Human Control
LEGEND D may suggest suppliers, reorder points, alerts, or decisions. Final approval remains with authorized users.
