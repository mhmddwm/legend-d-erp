# Core Engines

## Workflow Engine
Controls document lifecycle states.

## Revision Engine
Creates document revisions such as Rev.0, Rev.1, Rev.2.

## Audit Engine
Records who did what, when, and what changed.

## Notification Engine
Generates internal alerts and future email/WhatsApp notifications.

## Numbering Engine
Generates document numbers from centralized rules.

## Tax Engine
Calculates included and excluded taxes based on tax settings.

## Currency Engine
Prepares the system for multi-currency operations.

## Decision Engine
Scores and compares options, especially suppliers.

## AI Engine
Provides a future interface for AI analysis and recommendations.

## Event Bus
Publishes and consumes business events across modules.
