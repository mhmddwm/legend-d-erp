# AI Roadmap

## Phase 1 — AI Ready Data
- Structured audit events.
- Document timeline.
- Supplier quotation history.
- Decision log.
- Clean master data.

## Phase 2 — AI Insights
- Supplier recommendations.
- Stock risk alerts.
- Purchase cost analysis.
- Delay pattern detection.

## Phase 3 — AI Copilot
- Ask business questions in natural language.
- Explain supplier decisions.
- Generate procurement suggestions.

## Phase 4 — Company Brain
- Preserve business memory.
- Explain historical decisions.
- Understand relationships between suppliers, products, warehouses, documents, and payments.

## Phase 5 — Autonomous Suggestions
- Suggest PRs and RFQs.
- Suggest supplier selection.
- Suggest inventory replenishment.
- Human approval remains mandatory.
