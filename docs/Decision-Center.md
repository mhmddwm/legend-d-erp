# Decision Center Specification

The Decision Center is not just a price comparison table. It is a procurement decision dashboard.

## Inputs
- Supplier quotations.
- Tax treatment.
- Shipping cost.
- Lead time.
- Expected delivery date.
- Offer validity date.
- Payment terms.
- Supplier performance history.

## Outputs
- Total landed cost.
- Smart supplier score.
- Best price indicator.
- Fastest delivery indicator.
- Best payment terms indicator.
- AI recommendation text.
- Decision log.

## Decision Log
When a supplier is approved, the user should select reasons:
- Lowest price.
- Fastest delivery.
- Best payment terms.
- Preferred supplier.
- Best quality.
- Management decision.
- Other reason.
