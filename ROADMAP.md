# LEGEND D Platform Roadmap

## PF-01 — Platform Foundation
Status: In progress

Objectives:
- Organize the real project as a long-term platform, not a collection of screens.
- Add documentation, deployment, scripts, tests, and core-engine skeletons.
- Prepare the project for future AI, audit, workflow, revision, and decision intelligence.

## PF-02 — Procurement Intelligence
Objectives:
- RFQ Supplier Quotation Engine.
- Quotation Comparison Dashboard.
- Decision Center.
- Supplier Scoring Engine.
- Supplier Recommendation Engine.
- Decision Log.

## PF-03 — Purchase Order Engine
Objectives:
- Generate purchase orders from approved supplier quotation decisions.
- Preserve document traceability from PR → RFQ → Comparison → PO.

## PF-04 — Inventory Intelligence
Objectives:
- Stock prediction.
- Reorder suggestions.
- Movement timeline.
- Product AI memory.

## PF-05 — AI Copilot Foundation
Objectives:
- Company Brain.
- Natural language business questions.
- Procurement assistant.
- Inventory assistant.
